To apply to be an Individual Member of Farringdon Village Hall (a charitable incorporated organisation) please fill in this application form and press submit. Please complete a separate form for each individual member (not per residence). Membership is open at anytime, there is no cut-off date.

**Membership is FREE.**

But in the event that the Charity is dissolved whilst you are a Member or within 12 months thereafter you will pay a sum not exceeding £1 towards the cost of dissolution and any liabilities incurred by the Charity.

## Membership Application Form

By completing this form, you confirm that:

- You have read the [Constitution](/about/constitution/) of the Charity and accept its provisions
- You acknowledge that if the Charity is dissolved whilst you are a Member or within 12 months thereafter, you will pay a sum not exceeding £1 towards the cost of dissolution and any liabilities incurred by the Charity
- You agree to sign the Register of Members if your application is successful
- You consent to your details being held by the Charity for purposes of the Charity
- You confirm that you reside in or own a property in the Parish of Farringdon

### Privacy Notice

Your personal data will be held securely by Farringdon Village Hall (Charity Number: 1206624) solely for the purposes of charity membership administration. We will not share your data with third parties. You may request to see, amend, or delete your data at any time by contacting the Secretary. For more information, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).
